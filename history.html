Dice History

Invention Of Dice

Cubical dice with markings practically
equivalent to those of modern dice have
been found in Chinese excavations from
600 bce and in Egyptian tombs dating
from 2000 bce.

The first written records of
dice are found in the ancient
Sanskrit epic the Mahabharata,
composed in India more than
2,000 years ago.
